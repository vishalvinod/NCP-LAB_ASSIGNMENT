Details

The Cliffs at Princeville 7301 is a spacious 2-bedroom Kauai condo that encompasses over 1500 square feet of opulence , all dedicated to your comfort when you are note out exploring the splendors of Kauai's North Shore,the most luxuriant part of one the most beautiful islands of Hawaii.







A virtual home away from home, this condo in Kauai's Princeville resort and golf community features the following:

  • a large,breezy living room overlooking the Pacific Ocean
  • a complete kitchen
  • two bedrooms
  • two full baths
  • two private lanais to enjoy the tranquility of the island

This unit is large enough to accommodate up to five adults.


Continuously remodeled and upgraded, this unit, one of the best in the Five-star Cliffs at Princeville, Kauai condo resort, has never been nicer!

Golfers' Paradise!

Guests staying at this Princeville vacation rental condo are entitled to a substantial discount at one of the most magnificent golf courses in the world. What a deal!
